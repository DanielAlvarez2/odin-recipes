Maintenance Meal Prep Recipe

1400 Calories per day meal prep

This recipe is designed to maintain current body weight. It works out to roughly 1400 Calories per day. Makes four meals weighing about 10 ounces each, includes three bananas for snacks, and 10 ounces of iced tea mixed with 54 ounces of water.

Ingredients

  • Two 12.5oz cans of premium chunk white chicken meat in water[Price Chopper]
  • Two 8.5oz bags of Wholesome Pantry organic brown rice & lentils[ShopRite]
  • 10oz fresh salsa[Big Y]
  • Three small bananas around 16oz combined weight
  • 10oz Pure Leaf sweet iced tea
  • 54oz water

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken, rice, and salsa into 4 equal servings. Approximately 3.2oz chicken, 4.3oz rice, and 2.5oz salsa. Comes to about 10oz per serving. Ideally to be eaten 4 hours apart.
  2. Combine 10oz of iced tea with 54oz water and drink 1/4 with each meal
  3. Eat 1 banana 2 hours after each meal
